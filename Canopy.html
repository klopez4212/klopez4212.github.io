Canopy
2016

Canopy is an online education platform that equips aspiring stock traders with the right tools to enable them to make smart investments and to communicate with professionals about their trading strategies.

For this client project, we were tasked with designing the web responsive learning flow.

Design Process

User Research

Conducting user interviews allowed us to hone in on platform comprehension and persona validation. From this procedure, 3 main pain points were discovered.

Ideation

Top 5 initial Brainstorm ideas

1. Roadmap (levels, progress)

2. Dashboard (clean navigation, relevant news)

3. 5 min or shorter material at once

4. Summary, Cheat Sheet

5. Gamification (rewards, start with $10,000, fake $, $5/lesson)
