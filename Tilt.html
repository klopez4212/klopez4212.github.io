Tilt
2016

Client

Tilt is a crowdfunding company that lets groups collect, fundraise, or pool money online. Partnered with Live Nation and Creative Artists Agency, Tilt brings popular recording artists to college campus events around the country.

Problem

Even with existing funds, it's difficult for college students to discover and book artists to perform at their schools. Most college students don't know which artists are available; and even when they do, it's difficult to get in touch with them.

Role

I was responsible for creating the artist bio template page as well as the user experience of the entire product
